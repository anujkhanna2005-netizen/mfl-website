What is Part Truck Load?

Part Truck Load (PTL), also known as Less than Truck Load (LTL), is a freight model where multiple shippers share the capacity of a single truck. You pay only for the space your cargo occupies — making it significantly more cost-effective than booking an entire truck.

MFL's PTL operations run on fixed, scheduled routes between key cities. This predictability makes PTL ideal for regular, medium-volume shipments where cost optimization and consistent delivery schedules matter.

How PTL Works

Consolidated freight — simple, scheduled, and cost-efficient.

Book Your Space

Delivery & POD
