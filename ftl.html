What is Full Truck Load?

Full Truck Load (FTL) is a freight model where a single shipper books the entire cargo capacity of a truck. Your goods are the only cargo — the vehicle travels directly from origin to destination with no intermediate stops for other consignments.

FTL is ideal for large volumes, high-value shipments, or cargo requiring temperature control, fragile handling, or time-critical delivery. With MFL, FTL also means direct access to our operations team for complete visibility.

How FTL Works

From enquiry to delivery — a streamlined 4-step process.

1

Submit Your Enquiry

Share your origin, destination, cargo details, and preferred pickup date via call, WhatsApp, or our quote form.

2

Your shipment travels directly to the destination — no intermediate stops, no co-loading — and you receive delivery confirmation.

Why Choose FTL

The advantages of exclusive truck usage for your freight.

Dedicated Vehicle

Your cargo occupies the entire truck — no shared space, no interference from other consignments.

Zero Co-Loading

No intermediate loading or unloading. Your goods are sealed from origin to destination.

Faster Transit

Direct routes without collection stops mean significantly faster delivery windows.

Maximum Security

Fewer touchpoints, sealed loads, and cargo insurance on every movement.
