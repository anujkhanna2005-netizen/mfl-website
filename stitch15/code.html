Milestones that Define Us

Charting our journey from a single hub to a national logistics powerhouse.

2003

The Foundation

In 2003, MFL began as a humble dream with a single truck and a small office in Kanpur. Our founder, Mr. Arun Kumar Khanna, envisioned a logistics partner that prioritized integrity over volume. This single-truck operation laid the groundwork for what would become a national standard in reliability, proving that even the longest journeys begin with a single, committed step.

2005

Early Operations

By 2005, our reputation for punctuality had spread. We expanded our reach into major industrial corridors, becoming the trusted backbone for North India's textile and manufacturing sectors. This era was defined by the transition from a local carrier to a regional powerhouse, as we began connecting the industrial heartlands with unprecedented efficiency.

2010

Indore Hub Launch

Strategic entry into Central India. The Indore hub became our gateway to the South, enabling 48-hour delivery windows across major state lines.
