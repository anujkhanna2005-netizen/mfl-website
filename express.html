What is Express Freight?

MFL Express Freight is a priority-handling service designed for shipments where delivery timelines cannot be compromised. Express movements are treated with dedicated dispatch priority — your cargo gets first attention, first loading, and first departure.

Ideal for production-line emergencies, trade fair deadlines, urgent procurement, or any situation where standard transit windows aren't an option. Express freight is available as both dedicated (FTL) and consolidated (PTL) movements.

Coverage Indore–Kanpur corridor and 10+ connected cities Delivery Commitment 24–48 hours for distances under 800 km Priority Handling Dedicated vehicle, no consolidation Surcharge Applied over standard rates (quote on request) Dispatch Priority First loading priority on all active vehicles Booking Same-day booking accepted (subject to vehicle availability) Insurance Cargo insurance covered on all express movements
