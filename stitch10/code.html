Logistics Queries

Common questions about our forwarding operations.

What is the typical transit time between Kanpur and Indore? expand_more

For our standard corridor service, transit typically takes 36 to 48 hours depending on cargo type and specific loading schedules. We prioritize reliability over high-speed unrealistic promises.

How do I track my active shipment? expand_more

Once your shipment is booked, you'll receive a unique tracking ID. You can enter this on our 'Track' page for real-time location updates and estimated arrival windows.

Do you handle hazardous or fragile cargo? expand_more

Yes, we have specialized protocols for both fragile and specific classes of industrial cargo. Please detail your payload type in the inquiry form for a specialized handling quote.
