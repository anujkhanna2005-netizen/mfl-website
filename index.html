Frequent Operations Queries

Logistics & Transport FAQ

What is the standard freight cost per km?

Freight rates vary based on vehicle type (open truck vs. closed container) and payload. However, MFL guarantees transparent, highly competitive market rates natively without hidden surcharges. Contact our Kanpur or Indore hub for an exact, instant quote.

How long does Kanpur to Indore transport typically take?

Our flagship Kanpur ↔ Indore corridor operates on an expedited schedule. Standard transit times are precision-mapped, typically taking 24–48 hours depending on road constraints and loading protocols.

Do you provide tracking for critical shipments?

Yes, MFL utilizes GPS-monitored routes. Our centralized operations dispatch team provides real-time updates and coordinates tightly to ensure your consignment is tracked aggressively until it reaches the destination safely.

Do you provide transit insurance for high-value goods?

Yes, we facilitate comprehensive transit insurance coverage at nominal premiums. This ensures your high-value cargo is completely protected against unforeseen circumstances during inter-state haulage.

What is the minimum load capacity required?

While we specialize in Full Truck Load (FTL) bulk services for industrial manufacturers, we do accommodate Part Truck Load (PTL) shipments depending on the specific route and scheduling availability. Contact us with your exact tonnage.

How quickly are GST invoices generated post-delivery?

As a fully compliant transport corporation, MFL provides instant, computerized GST invoices immediately upon delivery consignment (POD) completion. Our internal accounting protocols manage all physical e-way bill clearances.
