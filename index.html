Frequent Operations Queries

Logistics & Transport FAQ

What is the standard freight cost per km?

Freight rates vary based on vehicle type and payload. However, MFL guarantees transparent, competitive market rates. Contact our team for an exact, instant quote.

How long does Kanpur to Indore transport typically take?

Our flagship Kanpur ↔ Indore corridor operates on an expedited schedule. Standard transit times typically take 24–48 hours depending on road conditions and loading protocols.

Do you provide tracking for critical shipments?

Yes, MFL utilizes GPS-monitored routes. Our team provides regular updates and coordinates tightly to ensure your consignment is handled safely until it reaches the destination.

Do you provide transit insurance for high-value goods?

Yes, we facilitate comprehensive transit insurance coverage at nominal premiums. This ensures your high-value cargo is completely protected during transport.

What is the minimum load capacity required?

While we specialize in Full Truck Load (FTL) bulk services, we do accommodate Part Truck Load (PTL) shipments depending on the route. Contact us with your tonnage requirements.

How quickly are GST invoices generated?

As a fully compliant transport corporation, MFL provides computerized GST invoices immediately upon delivery completion. Our team manages all e-way bill clearances.
