The story of Mamta Forwarding Logistics: a family-led commitment to reliable freight forwarding across India since 2003.

The MFL Journey

Mamta Forwarding Logistics began with a simple mission: to provide transparent, reliable, and secure freight services connecting the heart of India's manufacturing zones. What started as a modest regional operation in Indore has steadily evolved into an expansive logistics network serving over 20+ cities.

Despite our growth, we remain a family-led enterprise. This means our core values — integrity, accountability, and treating every cargo as if it were our own — are deeply embedded in every level of our operations.

Our Milestones

Key moments in the growth of MFL Logistics.

2003

Company Foundation

MFL is established in Indore by Mr. Arun Kumar Khanna with a vision to streamline regional freight operations and build reliable corridors across Central India.

2005

Early Operations

Secured key contracts with textile manufacturers across MP and FMCG distributors in Central India, building a strong reputation for zero-damage cargo handling and consistent on-time performance.

2010

Fleet Growth

Significant investment in expanding the proprietary fleet to manage increased demand across Central India and new freight corridors.

2018

Expansion to Kanpur

Opened the strategic Kanpur branch, connecting UP and Northern India to our primary network and extending our operational footprint.

2022

Technology Adoption

Implemented advanced route planning and operational tools to optimize efficiency and improve client communication across all active routes.

2025

Present Day & Future Growth

Serving 20+ cities with a commitment to continuous expansion, digital infrastructure, and delivering premium logistics experiences nationwide.
